Portland Argentinian Festival

501 (c) 3 Non Profit Organization

In 2019, our first festival drew over 1000 attendees with captivating dance and music performances, traditional food, and a surprise visit from Diego Valeri, making it an unforgettable celebration of culture and community.

Our Mission

We are a community of Argentinians who gather regularly to preserve our culture, customs, and traditions with the intention of sharing them with our families, friends, the people of Portland, and the surrounding areas.

Our Vission

We strive for the Portland Argentinian Festival to be a widely known event in the Portland area that is recognized by the Argentinian community in Oregon and throughout the United States, as a way of continuing to share our culture, customs, and traditions and preserve them for generations to come.
