Donate

We are looking for donations of all kinds! Here are some ways you can help us:

  • Donate $ through venmo @portlandargentinianfestival or send us a check to: Portland Argentinian Festival, 6953 SE Blaine St, Hillsboro OR 97123
  • Donate materials: foam board sheets, printing supplies, balloons, flags.
  • Donate gifts: We would love to raffle goodie baskets to gather funds! Please donate Argentinian wines and goods if possible.
  • Donate time: Can you print fliers at home? Send the invitation to 50 people? Please go to our volunteer page for more information
  • Amazon Wishlist
