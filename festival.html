Festival

In 2019, our first festival drew over 1000 attendees with captivating dance and music performances, traditional food, and a surprise visit from Diego Valeri, making it an unforgettable celebration of culture and community.

This year, we are gearing up for an extraordinary celebration of Argentine culture in the Pacific Northwest. The festival will take place at The Den PDX, a stunning and expansive event venue in SE Portland. Attendees can look forward to indulging in delicious local Argentine food from food carts, while being mesmerized by talented musicians and dancers. An art exhibit will showcase the beauty of Argentine art, and there will be a dedicated kids corner for our young guests to enjoy. Additionally, an interactive experience area promises to provide an immersive and engaging experience for all attendees. Get ready for a memorable and unique Argentine cultural experience!
