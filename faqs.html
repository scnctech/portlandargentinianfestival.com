FAQs

Is the event indoors or outdoors?

The event is primarily indoors but includes an enclosed exterior parking lot area with food carts and a dining area.

Is there FREE parking?

Our festival is located in an industrial neighborhood where all businesses are closed during the weekend, so there is plenty of FREE street parking in the area.

Can I bring my dog or any pet to the festival?

Pets are not permitted, but certified service animals are always welcome.

Is this an event for all ages?

The Portland Argentinian Festival is for ALL AGES. There is fun for the whole family!
IDs will be checked, and bracelets will be used for those 21 and older.

Will alcoholic beverages be sold?

Only people 21+ can purchase and consume alcohol on the premises. Outside alcohol is not permitted. IDs will be checked.

How much does it cost to attend the Portland Argentinian Festival?

  • Tickets will be only sold at the door
  • $10 Adults 18+
  • $5 Seniors 65+
  • 17 and under FREE

What does my ticket purchase include?

Your admission includes our main stage shows, workshops, art exhibits, and a day of fun.
Food and beverages are not included.
Your purchase covers the costs of venue rental and miscellaneous event expenses.

How do I get to the Portland Argentinian Festival by public transport?

We recommend you use TriMet's Trip Planner by clicking HERE.
The venue's address is 819 SE Taylor St, Portland, OR 97214 (JaJaPDX).

Is this event cash-only?

The ticket booth and vendors prefer cash, but tickets can also be purchased with a credit or debit card. Most vendors will have the ability to charge cards. There is NO ATM in the venue.

Is smoking permitted on the premises?

No smoking or vaping of any kind is allowed in the venue.

Are there restrooms in the venue?

Yes, the venue has one restroom indoors and two porta potties outside.

What are the rules and regulations?

  • No outside food or drinks
  • No weapons (guns, knives, or anything that can be used as a weapon) are allowed on the premises.
  • No offensive attire or paraphernalia will be allowed (political, religious, etc.)
  • This is a private event. We reserve the right to refuse service or admission to anyone for any reason.
  • We will check bags at the entrance for your safety.
  • No nudity.
  • Only service animals allowed.
